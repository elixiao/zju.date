WebStorm正则替换

  1. 驼峰转下划线\l

    查找: ([A-Z])
    替换: _\l$1

    someThing -> some_thing

  2. 大写转小写\L

    查找: (\w*)
    替换: \L$1

    SOMETHING -> something

  3. 小写转大写\U

    查找: (\w*)
    替换: \U$1

    something -> SOMETHING

  1. 首字母小写\l

    查找: (\w*)
    替换: \l$1

    Something -> something

  2. 首字母大写\u

    查找: (\w*)
    替换: \u$1

    something -> Something

  3. 多行匹配

    查找: <div id="id">([\s\S]*?)</div>
    替换: <span>\1</span>

    <div id="id">something</div> -> <span>something<span>
